Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born Philadelphia, Pennysylvania August 23
  • 1996 - NBA draft 13th pick to Charlotte Hornets.He co-signed a three-year, $3.5 million rookie contract.
  • 1997 - Slam dunk contest champion.
  • 1999-2002 - Phil Jackson arrives and the three-peat.
  • 2004 - Fallout with Shaq, Phil Jackson leaves.
  • 2005 - Phil Jackson returns
  • 2006 - Career-high 81 points
  • 2008 - Olympian
  • 2008-2010 - Return to championship form
  • 2012 - Final Olympic medal
  • 2016 - Dear Basketball/Retirement
  • 2017 - Jersey retirement(No 8 and 24)
  • 2018 - Oscar
  • 2018 - Death in helicopter crash.
